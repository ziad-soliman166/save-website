ABOUT SAVE


SAVE is a non-profit organization which started 11 years ago.
We have medical students who are specialized in
their field. SAVE aims to raise medical awareness and
teach people the principles of first aid and some
necessary medical information to overcome some well-known misconceptions.

SAVE specifically targets age groups ranging from 18 to above.
This process is done by Academic and public health Committiees who are trained in this topics and
simplify the information as much as possible.

OUR VISION


To create generations of well-rounded youth and
adolescents who have adequate knowledge that makes
them able to have healthy lifestyles and help themselves
and community members in case of emergency and
correct the medical misconceptions that are rooted in our society.

OUR GOALS


- Improve their self-confidence and communication skills.
- Increase parents' awareness about their children's mental and physical health.
- Making use of youth's passion in society to learn and increase their medical knowledge

OUR MISSION


- Making Campaigns Externally with other Organizations to achieve our goal of spreading Awareness.
- Organizing events that have medical sessions, workshops and soft skills sessions.
- Improving awareness through the main project that is held in
- the summer has many medical interactive Sessions and workshops and soft skills sessions that is needed in their real life.
- Giving interactive sessions and workshops for more efficiency.
- Organizing Health Fair and sketches for them to improve their self confidence and Communication skills.
